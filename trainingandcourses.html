1 Hour training rate

Bespoke 35 euro

By the end of the training you will have completed from design to finish two pieces minimum. Firstly with lead and then a second piece using the Tiffany method. We may find time to make an additional Tiffany piece. Upon completion of the training you will be capable of designing and making your own work either at your home or back in my workshop under my supervision should you wish to remain engaged. I run a five day course from 9am through to 5pm with an hour or more for lunch. There will be tea making facilities and juice etc on hand. This training is a great opportunity for you to develop a new skill from which you can make and sell your work. Alternatively just make gifts and trinkets for family and friends, I will train you to ensure you are capable of both and you can recoup your course fee in no time…… look out next years Christmas market……. Day One - 9am to 12h30 Health and safety aspects of working with Glass, Lead, Solder, Flux and associated chemicals. Safety in the workshop and associated equipment, fans, oven, blades……..

1 Day training rate

Bespoke 09h00 - 17h00 240 euro

Introduction to Glass Types of glass and how they differ What we use for our craft Introduction to Lead and copper foil Types and compositions Structural differences, what to use, when and why Sizes and shapes Fluxes Creating your pattern or as they say in the USA, your cartoon. Considering structure of the lead, the strength, what will work and what doesn’t work? Where to cut and join, how to size and more? The drawing, Lead or Tiffany, tracing and making the master process. Talk about Apps, glass painting, glass printing and other methods of colouring the glass.

The tools in the workshop Pens : Horseshoe nails Geometry pieces Light table / Construction table / Glues / Boards Cutters circular, angled, straight / Snips Grinders / lead cutters / Bandsaws Open the toolbox ………The list goes on Hands on

5 Day residential training course

1000 euro
5 days training 1000 euro per student, this includes
  • Maximum of ONLY 2 students at any one time.
  • All materials used throughout the course. Glass, lead, and so on.
  • Should you run out of time and need to work Saturday morning, I will run that session free of charge. I want you to leave having achieved your goals and being satisfied with your new craft skills.
  • Accommodation in the Riverstones gite will vary on price as per requirements.
  • Fully contained apartment with kitchen, en suite, mezzanine bedroom, and living area.
  • Double bedroom with en suite.
  • Two single beds room with en suite.
  • Post-course support and further training. I will be on hand through calls, video calls, and text should you need advice and guidance.
  • My workshop is available to use under my supervision at a rate of 35 euros per hour, pending availability. This time can be used to learn more or simply make pieces, be it lead or Tiffany.
  • There will be a charge for materials in this scenario, to be agreed upon when your piece is designed on paper.
